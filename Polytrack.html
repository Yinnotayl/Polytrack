Gameplay Mechanics

The objective is simple: complete each track as quickly as possible. The game features 13 unique tracks filled with loops, jumps, and technical sections designed to challenge your driving skills.

For creative players, the built-in level editor allows complete freedom to design custom tracks using ramps, loops, and other elements. These tracks can then be shared with other players.

How to Play

  1. Select a Track — Choose an official track or create one in the editor.
  2. Customize Your Vehicle — Personalize your car’s appearance.
  3. Race Against the Clock — Drive through obstacles and aim for the fastest time.

Controls

Driving

  • Accelerate — W / Up Arrow
  • Brake / Reverse — S / Down Arrow
  • Steer Left — A / Left Arrow
  • Steer Right — D / Right Arrow
  • Restart Track — R / Enter

Editor

  • Place Elements — Left Mouse
  • Rotate View — Middle Mouse + Move
  • Pan View — Right Mouse + Move
  • Zoom — Mouse Wheel
  • Adjust Height — Shift + Scroll or Z / C
  • Rotate Piece — R / Space
  • Delete Piece — X
  • Test Track — T

Tips

Study the Track

Learn where the turns, jumps, and obstacles are before attempting a fast run.

Follow the Racing Line

Take the smoothest path through corners to maintain maximum speed.

Use Track Features Properly

  • Approach loops and jumps with enough speed.
  • Brake slightly before sharp corners to avoid crashes.

Practice Runs

Replaying tracks helps refine your timing and improve your racing line.

Experiment With the Editor

Building tracks helps you understand how obstacles affect movement and momentum.
